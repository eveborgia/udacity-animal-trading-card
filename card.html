Sphynx Cat

The Sphynx cat is a breed of cat known for its lack of coat. It was developed through selective breeding, starting in the 1960s. The skin should have the texture of chamois, as it has fine hairs.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Origin: Toronto
  • Higher Classification: Cat
  • Rank: Breed

Hairless cat breeds present definite care challenges for their owners, such as dealing with the oils on the skin.
